The Journey

I started my journey as a BCA student with a hunger to go beyond the syllabus. While most were learning syntax, I was obsessing over how to solve friction in local commerce.

This led to the creation of VardhanFlow—my first step from developer to founder. I believe engineering isn't just about writing code; it's about building "Flow" in businesses that need it most.

From winning hackathons like Lion's Den 2.0 to launching a live SaaS, I've proven that a student with a vision can engineer a business ecosystem.
